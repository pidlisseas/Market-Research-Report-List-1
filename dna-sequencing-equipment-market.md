<p><strong>The DNA Sequencing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing</li><li>Next Generation DNA Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DNA sequencing equipment market includes two primary types: Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing and Next Generation DNA Sequencing (NGS). Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing, also known as Sanger sequencing, relies on chain-terminating dideoxynucleotides to determine the nucleotide sequence of DNA fragments. In contrast, NGS employs advanced technologies to simultaneously sequence millions of DNA strands, enabling high-throughput and cost-effective analysis. These methods cater to diverse applications in genomics, diagnostics, and personalized medicine, driving innovation and growth within the biotechnology sector.</p></p>
