<p><p>The thermal radiation infrared light source market is categorized into three types: near-infrared, mid-infrared, and far-infrared. Near-infrared focuses on wavelengths from approximately 0.7 to 1.5 micrometers, commonly used in telecommunications and medical applications. Mid-infrared, ranging from 1.5 to 5 micrometers, is utilized in spectroscopy and sensing technologies. Far-infrared, extending beyond 5 micrometers, finds applications in heating systems and thermal imaging. Each type serves distinct industrial and scientific purposes, driving advancements in technology and applications.</p></p>
