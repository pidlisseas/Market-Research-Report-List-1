<p><strong>The Geared Elevator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isokinetic Operation</li><li>Frequency Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geared elevator market can be categorized into isokinetic operation and frequency conversion types. Isokinetic operation maintains a constant speed by adjusting motor torque according to load variations, ensuring smooth and efficient performance. Meanwhile, frequency conversion utilizes variable frequency drives to control motor speed, enhancing energy efficiency and allowing for precise speed adjustments. Both technologies aim to improve the operational efficiency and sustainability of geared elevators, catering to various building requirements and user preferences in modern infrastructure.</p></p>
