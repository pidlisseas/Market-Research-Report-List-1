<p><ul><li>Wax Element Thermostatic Valve Core</li><li>Shape Memory Alloys Thermostatic Valve Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermostatic mixers market primarily features two types of valve cores: wax element thermostatic valve cores and shape memory alloys (SMA) thermostatic valve cores. Wax element valve cores utilize the expansion properties of wax to regulate temperature, providing reliable performance in water mixing applications. In contrast, SMA valve cores leverage the unique elasticity of shape memory alloys, allowing for precise temperature control with rapid response times. Both types enhance comfort and safety in plumbing and industrial systems by maintaining consistent water temperatures.</p></p>
