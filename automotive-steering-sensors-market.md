<p><strong>The Automotive Steering Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contacting</li><li>Magnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive steering sensors market is primarily divided into two types: contacting and magnetic sensors. Contacting sensors utilize physical connection with the steering column, providing direct feedback on angular position and torque, often ensuring precise control. Magnetic sensors, on the other hand, rely on magnetic fields to determine the position and movement of the steering wheel, offering advantages such as durability and reduced wear over time. Both types are essential for enhancing vehicle safety and performance through advanced steering systems.</p></p>
