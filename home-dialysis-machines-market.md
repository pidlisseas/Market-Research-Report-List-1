<p><strong>The Home Dialysis Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peritoneal Dialysis</li><li>Hemodialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home dialysis machines market is primarily segmented into two types: Peritoneal Dialysis (PD) and Hemodialysis (HD). Peritoneal Dialysis involves the use of a catheter implanted in the abdomen, allowing for dialysis solution to wash out toxins. Hemodialysis, on the other hand, requires a machine that filters blood through an artificial kidney, typically involving more frequent sessions. Both modalities offer patients flexibility, convenience, and improved quality of life, driving growth in the home dialysis market.</p></p>
