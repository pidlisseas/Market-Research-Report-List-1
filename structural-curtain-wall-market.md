<p><p>A structural curtain wall is a non-structural façade system that spans multiple floors of a building, typically made from glass and metal. It serves as a protective exterior barrier, allowing natural light to enter while providing insulation and weather resistance. The design focuses on aesthetics and functionality, relying on the building's frame for support rather than the curtain wall itself.</p><p>The Structural Curtain Wall Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing urbanization, a rise in commercial construction, and a growing emphasis on energy-efficient building solutions. Trends in the market include advancements in materials and manufacturing technologies, leading to improved performance and sustainability. Innovative designs, such as unitized systems and dynamic facades, are gaining traction, allowing for enhanced customization and quicker installation processes. </p><p>Additionally, there is a significant push towards integrating smart technologies into curtain walls, enabling features like energy management and automated shading systems. As green building initiatives become more prevalent, the demand for environmentally friendly materials in curtain wall designs is also on the rise, further shaping the market landscape.</p></p>
