<p><p>The Infectious Diseases Molecular Diagnostics Kit market comprises various technologies for detecting pathogens. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies specific DNA sequences, enabling rapid identification of infectious agents. Molecular hybridization involves the binding of nucleic acid probes to target sequences, facilitating the detection of infections. Biochips utilize microarrays for simultaneous testing of multiple pathogens, offering high-throughput capabilities. Other techniques may include isothermal amplification and next-generation sequencing, expanding the diagnostic possibilities for infectious diseases. Together, these methods enhance accuracy and speed in disease detection.</p></p>
