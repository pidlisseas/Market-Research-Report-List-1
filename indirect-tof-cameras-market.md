<p><p>The indirect Time-of-Flight (ToF) cameras market comprises several types, primarily including Modulated Light ToF Cameras and Phase-Shift ToF Cameras. Modulated Light ToF Cameras utilize light pulses to measure distance by analyzing the time delay of reflected light, enabling precise depth mapping. Phase-Shift ToF Cameras, on the other hand, determine distance by measuring the phase difference between emitted and received light waves, offering high accuracy in complex environments. Other types may include specialized cameras tailored for specific applications, enhancing overall market diversity.</p></p>
