<p><strong>The Movement Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Movement Sensors</li><li>Passive Movement Sensors</li><li>Dual Technology Movement Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The movement sensors market is divided into three primary types: Active, Passive, and Dual Technology sensors. Active movement sensors emit signals, like infrared or ultrasonic waves, to detect motion based on reflections. Passive movement sensors, on the other hand, rely on detecting changes in energy, such as heat from moving objects. Dual Technology movement sensors combine both active and passive methods, enhancing accuracy and reducing false alarms by requiring both types of detection to trigger an alert.</p></p>
