<p><strong>The Sterile Medical Paper Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure Paper Packaging</li><li>Blister Paper Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterile medical paper packaging market comprises two main types: pure paper packaging and blister paper packaging. Pure paper packaging typically utilizes multi-layered paper materials that provide sufficient barrier properties to protect sterility. It is often used for items like surgical instruments and dressings. On the other hand, blister paper packaging combines paper with plastic components to create sealed cavities for medical devices, offering visibility and ease of access while maintaining sterility. Both types are essential for ensuring safe and hygienic medical product delivery.</p></p>
