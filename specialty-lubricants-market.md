Mineral oil-based lubricants, sourced from crude oil, are widely used for their cost-effectiveness and reliability. Synthetic oil-based lubricants are engineered chemicals designed for enhanced performance, offering superior temperature stability, reduced friction, and longer life, making them ideal for high-demand applications. Each type addresses specific industry needs and environmental concerns.</p></p>
