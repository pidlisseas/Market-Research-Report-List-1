<p><strong>The Surgical Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground Referenced Generators</li><li>Isolated Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical generators market comprises two主要类型: Ground Referenced Generators and Isolated Generators. Ground Referenced Generators are linked to a common earth ground, minimizing electrical interference but posing potential safety risks if grounding is compromised. In contrast, Isolated Generators provide a higher level of safety by offering electrical isolation from the ground, reducing the risk of electric shock. Both types serve critical roles in surgical procedures, facilitating precise thermal tissue cutting and coagulation while prioritizing patient safety.</p></p>
