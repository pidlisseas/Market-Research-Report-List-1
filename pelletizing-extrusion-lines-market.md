<p><p>Pelletizing extrusion lines are classified mainly into vertical and horizontal types, each serving distinct manufacturing needs. Vertical extrusion lines are typically compact and efficient, allowing for easier maintenance and reduced floor space usage, making them suitable for smaller production facilities. In contrast, horizontal extrusion lines offer higher throughputs, making them ideal for large-scale operations. Both types effectively convert raw materials into uniform pellets, but their design and capacity cater to different operational requirements and production volumes in the market.</p></p>
