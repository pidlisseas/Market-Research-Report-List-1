<p><p>The osteochondral implants market includes various types, primarily categorized into allografts, screws, and plates. Allografts involve transplanting cartilage and bone tissue from a donor to restore damaged areas. Screw implants are typically used to fixate osteochondral grafts securely in place within the joint. Plates, on the other hand, provide stability and support to the cartilage repair site, ensuring proper integration and healing. Together, these solutions aim to enhance joint function and alleviate pain caused by osteochondral defects.</p></p>
