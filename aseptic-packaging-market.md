<p><p>Aseptic packaging is a method that ensures the sterility of food and beverages by packaging them in a sterile environment. This process involves sterilizing both the product and the packaging materials separately before filling and sealing them in a sterile atmosphere, which significantly extends shelf life without the need for preservatives. This technology is especially popular for liquid food products like juices, soups, and sauces.</p><p>The Aseptic Packaging Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 2.41% during the forecast period, driven by increasing consumer demand for convenient and long-lasting food options.
