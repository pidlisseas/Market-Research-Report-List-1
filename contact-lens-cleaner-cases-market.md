<p><p>The contact lens cleaner cases market encompasses three main types: physical shock, ultrasonic cleaning, and protein electrophoresis cleaning. Physical shock cleaners utilize vibrations to dislodge debris from lenses. Ultrasonic cleaning employs high-frequency sound waves to create microscopic bubbles that gently clean lenses without damage. Protein electrophoresis cleaning targets protein buildup on lenses, using electrical charges to separate and remove contaminants effectively. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various consumer preferences for maintaining lens hygiene and clarity.</p></p>
