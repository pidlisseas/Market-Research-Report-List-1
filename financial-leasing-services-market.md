<p><p>Financial leasing services encompass various market types, primarily including capital leases and operating leases. A capital lease involves long-term asset usage with ownership benefits, making it akin to a purchase, as it typically cannot be canceled without severe penalties. In contrast, an operating lease is short-term, allowing users to rent assets without assuming ownership risks or responsibilities. Other financial leasing options may include sale-and-leaseback arrangements or cross-border leases, catering to different financial needs and asset management strategies for businesses.</p></p>
