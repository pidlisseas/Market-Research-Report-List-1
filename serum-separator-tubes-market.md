<p><p>The Serum Separator Tubes (SST) market is segmented into two main types: glass and plastic. Glass SSTs are favored for their inert nature, ensuring minimal interaction with serum, thus providing accurate test results. However, they are more fragile and heavier. In contrast, plastic SSTs are lighter, shatter-resistant, and more convenient for transportation and handling. Each type serves specific laboratory needs, with glass typically preferred for long-term storage and plastic being advantageous for cost-effectiveness and ease of use in routine testing.</p></p>
