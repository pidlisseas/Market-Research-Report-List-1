<p><strong>The Slim Range Hood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top Suction</li><li>Side Suction</li><li>Downdraft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Slim range hoods come in three primary types: top suction, side suction, and downdraft. Top suction models are installed above the cooking surface, efficiently capturing smoke and odors directly above. Side suction hoods mount on the wall or cabinetry, drawing air from the sides and ideal for tight spaces. Downdraft hoods rise from the countertop when needed and retract when not, offering a sleek design while providing effective ventilation, especially in open kitchen layouts. Each type addresses specific kitchen needs and aesthetics.</p></p>
