<p><p>The System on Module (SOM) market is categorized primarily into three types: ARM architecture, x86 architecture, and other architectures. ARM-based SOMs are favored for their power efficiency and compact design, making them ideal for mobile and embedded applications. x86 architecture SOMs offer higher processing power and compatibility with legacy systems, making them suitable for industrial and computing applications. Other markets encompass specialized architectures, such as MIPS or RISC-V, catering to niche applications like IoT and custom hardware solutions.</p></p>
