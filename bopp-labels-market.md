<p><p>The BOPP labels market comprises several types, including pressure-sensitive labels, shrink labels, wet glue labels, and others. Pressure-sensitive labels feature an adhesive that activates on contact, providing easy application on various surfaces. Shrink labels conform to the shape of the container when heat is applied, offering a tamper-evident and decorative option. Wet glue labels require adhesive application during the labeling process, often used on bottles. Other types may include eco-friendly options or specialty labels for niche markets, catering to diverse packaging needs.</p></p>
