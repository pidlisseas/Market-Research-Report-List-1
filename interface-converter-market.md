<p><p>The interface converter market is primarily divided into two categories based on fiber types: singlemode and multimode interface converters. Singlemode interface converters are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing narrow light wavelengths to minimize signal loss, making them ideal for expansive networks. In contrast, multimode interface converters are used for shorter distances and employ a broader light path, accommodating multiple light modes. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring efficient data transmission in various network environments, tailored to specific infrastructure needs.</p></p>
