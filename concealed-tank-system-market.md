<p><p>The concealed tank system market is segmented into two main types: dual flush and single flush systems. Dual flush systems offer two flushing options—one for liquid waste and another for solid waste—promoting water efficiency and conservation. In contrast, single flush systems provide a single, consistent flush volume, which is simpler but less versatile in managing water use. Both types are designed for modern bathroom aesthetics, hiding the tank behind the wall while ensuring effective waste management and enhanced design.</p></p>
