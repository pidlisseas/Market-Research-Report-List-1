<p><p>The Food & Beverage Plastics Market comprises various types of plastics primarily used for packaging. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is favored for its strength and recyclability, ideal for bottles. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is known for its durability and is commonly used for containers. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) offers flexibility but faces scrutiny for environmental concerns. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is lightweight and often used for bags, while PP (Polypropylene) provides heat resistance, suitable for food containers. PS (Polystyrene) is economical but less eco-friendly, often seen in disposable items.</p></p>
