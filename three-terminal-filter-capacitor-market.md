<p><ul><li>π-type Filter</li><li>T-type Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Three Terminal Filter Capacitor market primarily features two types: π-type and T-type filters. The π-type filter consists of two capacitors and an inductor, effectively reducing ripple voltage in power supplies, making it suitable for high-frequency applications. In contrast, the T-type filter features two inductors and a capacitor, offering improved response and reduced output noise. Both filter types are essential in various electronic applications, including telecommunications and power management systems, ensuring stable performance and signal integrity.</p></p>
