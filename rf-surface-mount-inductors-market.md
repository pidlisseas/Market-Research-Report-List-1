<p><strong>The RF Surface Mount Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire Wound Type</li><li>Film Type</li><li>Multilayer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF Surface Mount Inductors market includes three main types: Wire Wound, Film, and Multilayer. Wire Wound inductors utilize coiled wire to achieve inductance, offering high performance and stability. Film type inductors, made from thin-film technology, provide a compact size and lower inductance values, suitable for high-frequency applications. Multilayer inductors consist of multiple layers of conductive and insulating materials, delivering high inductance in a small footprint, ideal for modern miniaturized electronic devices. Each type serves distinct applications in RF circuits.</p></p>
