<p><p>The ceiling-mounted PIR detector market includes three main types: occupancy detectors, presence detectors, and motion detectors. Occupancy detectors identify when a space is occupied, activating lighting or HVAC systems based on occupancy status. Presence detectors are more sensitive, detecting even minimal movement to ensure energy efficiency and safety in areas where people may linger. Motion detectors focus on detecting larger movements, typically for security purposes, triggering alarms or notifications when movement is sensed in a designated area.</p></p>
