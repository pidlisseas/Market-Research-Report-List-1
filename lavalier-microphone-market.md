<p><strong>The Lavalier Microphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Omnidirectional</li><li>Cardioid</li><li>Omni</li><li>Supercardioid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lavalier microphone market features various types distinguished by their pickup patterns. Omnidirectional mics capture sound equally from all directions, making them ideal for general use. Cardioid microphones focus on sound from the front while minimizing side and rear noise, suitable for interviews. Supercardioid mics have a narrower pickup range with some sensitivity to the rear, often used in live performances. Each type is designed for specific applications, catering to different user needs in broadcasting, film, and event production.</p></p>
