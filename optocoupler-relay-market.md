<p><p>The optocoupler relay market is characterized by various output types, notably FET (Field-Effect Transistor) and SCR (Silicon Controlled Rectifier) outputs. FET output optocouplers are known for their fast switching speeds, low power consumption, and high input impedance, making them suitable for digital signals. In contrast, SCR output optocouplers handle higher power loads, providing robust performance in applications requiring greater voltage and current control. Both types serve essential roles in isolating and controlling electrical signals in various industries.</p></p>
