<p><strong>The Galvanized Iron Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Galvanized Iron Wire</li><li>Cold Galvanized Iron Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized iron wire market is primarily divided into two types: hot galvanized iron wire and cold galvanized iron wire. Hot galvanized iron wire is coated with zinc through a hot-dip process, providing superior corrosion resistance and durability, making it ideal for outdoor applications. In contrast, cold galvanized iron wire is coated through an electroplating process, offering less protection but at a lower cost, suitable for indoor use and lighter applications. Both types serve various industries, including construction, agriculture, and fencing.</p></p>
