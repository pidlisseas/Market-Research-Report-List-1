<p><p>The commercial chillers market encompasses various types, including centrifugal, scroll, screw, and others. Centrifugal chillers are efficient for large capacities, utilizing a rotating impeller for cooling. Scroll chillers are compact and suitable for smaller applications, featuring two spiral scrolls for refrigerant compression. Screw chillers employ twin-screw compressors for high efficiency and reliability in medium to large-scale operations. Other types may include absorption chillers and reciprocating chillers, catering to diverse commercial needs and varying operational demands.</p></p>
