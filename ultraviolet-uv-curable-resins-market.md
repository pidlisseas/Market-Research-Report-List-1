<p><strong>The Ultraviolet (UV) Curable Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne UV Resins</li><li>100% Solids UV Resins</li><li>Waterborne UV Resins</li><li>Powder UV Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) curable resins market consists of several types: </p><p>1. **Solventborne UV Resins** contain solvents that evaporate during curing, resulting in a durable finish.</p><p>2. **100% Solids UV Resins** have no volatile components, providing high performance and eco-friendliness.</p><p>3. **Waterborne UV Resins** use water as a primary solvent, offering low VOC emissions and easier handling.</p><p>4. **Powder UV Resins** are applied as a powder and cured using UV light, producing strong coatings without solvents. All types cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
