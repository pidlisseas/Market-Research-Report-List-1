<p><p>The Solid Oxide Fuel Cell (SOFC) market can be categorized into three main types: planar, tubular, and others. Planar SOFCs are characterized by flat structures, offering high power density and compact designs, making them suitable for various applications. Tubular SOFCs feature cylindrical shapes, providing better thermal stability and resilience at high temperatures, ideal for large-scale power generation. Other SOFC types include intermediate temperature and micro-scale designs, catering to niche markets and specialized applications, enhancing energy efficiency and versatility.</p></p>
