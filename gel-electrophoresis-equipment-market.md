<p><p>The gel electrophoresis equipment market is segmented into two primary types: horizontal and vertical systems. Horizontal gel electrophoresis is commonly used for separating nucleic acids, featuring a flat, rectangular gel setup that allows for easy viewing and cutting. In contrast, vertical gel electrophoresis is typically employed for protein separation, utilizing a gel that is oriented vertically to achieve higher resolution and facilitate sharper band separation. Both systems cater to specific applications in molecular biology and biochemistry laboratories.</p></p>
