<p><strong>The BLDC Electric Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Motor</li><li>Outer Rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The BLDC (Brushless DC) electric motor market is primarily categorized into two types: inner rotor motors and outer rotor motors. Inner rotor motors feature the rotor inside the stator, offering high torque and compact designs, making them suitable for applications requiring efficient power delivery in limited spaces. Conversely, outer rotor motors have the rotor on the outside, providing larger surface areas for heat dissipation and enhanced torque density, making them ideal for applications such as fans and electric vehicles.</p></p>
