<p><p>Flight conveyors are mechanical systems used to transport materials within manufacturing and distribution processes. The horizontal type moves products along a flat or slightly inclined path, ideal for transporting bulk items over longer distances. In contrast, inclined flight conveyors are designed to move materials between different elevation levels, facilitating vertical transport. Both types enhance operational efficiency, reduce labor costs, and can be customized for various applications, making them essential in industries such as food processing, packaging, and assembly.</p></p>
