<p><p>The Critical Care Information System (CCIS) market is divided into two primary types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based solutions. On-Premise systems are installed locally within a healthcare facility, providing full control over data security and customization, but often require significant initial investment and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based systems offer flexibility and scalability, enabling remote access to patient data and easier updates. These systems typically have lower upfront costs and facilitate collaboration among healthcare professionals but may raise concerns about data privacy and security.</p></p>
