<p><p>The flame retardant textile market is divided into two main types: essential flame retardant fibers and modified flame retardant fibers. Essential flame retardant fibers are inherently resistant to combustion, providing built-in protection without chemical treatment, making them ideal for high-risk applications. Conversely, modified flame retardant fibers undergo chemical treatment to enhance their fire resistance characteristics. This modification allows for versatility in performance and aesthetics while ensuring compliance with safety standards in various industries, including automotive, construction, and apparel.</p></p>
