Non-linear assets can include fixed machinery or buildings, demanding periodic maintenance. Field Service Management (FSM) focuses on managing mobile resources and service technicians in the field. Assets Maintenance, Repair, and Operations (MRO) market revolves around optimizing the upkeep and functionality of assets, ensuring they operate efficiently and effectively throughout their lifecycle, reducing downtime and costs.</p></p>
