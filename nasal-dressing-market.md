<p><p>The nasal dressing market is categorized into two primary types: bioresorbable and non-absorbable. Bioresorbable nasal dressings are designed to naturally dissolve within the body over time, eliminating the need for removal and reducing the risk of complications. In contrast, non-absorbable nasal dressings are intended for longer-term use and require surgical removal after their purpose is served. Each type serves distinct patient needs, with bioresorbable options emphasizing convenience and safety, while non-absorbable dressings offer durability and prolonged support.</p></p>
