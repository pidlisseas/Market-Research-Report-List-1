<p><p>The automotive power steering cooler market is categorized into three main types: tube type coolers, tank type coolers, and others. Tube type coolers feature a series of tubes that allow fluid to flow, enhancing heat dissipation. Tank type coolers utilize a reservoir design that offers a larger surface area for cooling. "Others" encompasses alternative designs such as integrated coolers or modular systems. Each type serves to maintain optimal fluid temperatures, thereby improving steering performance and prolonging system life.</p></p>
