Oscilloscopes with a sampling rate of less than 1 GS/s are typically used for general-purpose applications, offering sufficient performance for basic signal analysis. In contrast, oscilloscopes exceeding 1 GS/s cater to advanced applications, including high-speed electronic testing, where detailed signal behavior and rapid transient events must be accurately captured and analyzed.</p></p>
