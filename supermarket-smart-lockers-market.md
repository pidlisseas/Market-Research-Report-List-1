<p><p>Supermarket smart lockers are categorized into three main types based on temperature settings: ambient lockers, chilled lockers, and frozen lockers. Ambient lockers are designed for standard grocery items that do not require refrigeration. Chilled lockers maintain a cooler environment suitable for perishable goods like dairy and fresh produce. Frozen lockers operate at sub-zero temperatures, ideal for frozen foods and ice cream. These lockers enhance convenience, allowing customers to securely collect their purchases while ensuring proper storage conditions for various products.</p></p>
