<p><p>The flow wrap machine market can be categorized into horizontal and vertical types. Horizontal flow wrap machines operate with products placed flat, typically suited for items like bars, snacks, or baked goods. In contrast, vertical flow wrap machines handle products in an upright position, ideal for bags of granules, liquids, or fragile items.
