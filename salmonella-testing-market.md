Monoclonal antibodies specifically bind to Salmonella antigens, enabling detection through immunoassays, which are widely used for their sensitivity. Immunoassays include enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) and lateral flow tests. Differential light scattering methods analyze changes in light patterns when bacteria are present.
