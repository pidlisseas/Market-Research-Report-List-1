<p><p>Culture media can be categorized into several types based on their composition and intended application. Classic media are traditional nutrient solutions supporting general cell growth, often enriched with serum. Serum-free media eliminate serum for simplified and standardized cell culture, catering to specific cell types and reducing variability. Stem cell culture media are specialized formulations designed to maintain the pluripotency and differentiation potential of stem cells. Additionally, other markets include tailored media for specific cell lines or specialized applications, enhancing experimental outcomes.</p></p>
