<p><p>The Intelligent Buoy System market encompasses three main types of sonobuoys: active, passive, and special purpose. Active sonobuoys emit sound waves to detect underwater objects, aiding in submarine tracking. Passive sonobuoys listen for sounds in the environment, making them useful for monitoring marine life or detecting vessels without revealing their own presence. Special purpose sonobuoys are designed for specific applications, such as environmental monitoring or research. Together, these technologies enhance maritime surveillance and underwater exploration capabilities.</p></p>
