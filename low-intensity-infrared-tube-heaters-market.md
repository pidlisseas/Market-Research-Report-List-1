<p><p>Low Intensity Infrared Tube Heaters are categorized into two main market types: Single-Stage and Two-Stage. Single-Stage heaters operate at a constant output level, providing consistent heating suitable for smaller spaces or applications requiring steady temperatures. In contrast, Two-Stage heaters offer adjustable heating levels, allowing users to switch between high and low outputs for varying heating needs, enhancing energy efficiency and versatility. Both types cater to different applications in industrial, commercial, and agricultural settings, optimizing thermal comfort and operational costs.</p></p>
